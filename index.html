Our services

Environmental and Social Safeguards Engineering and Risk Management (ESSERM) Consult has the expertise and the capabilities in the areas the following;

Environmental and Social Risks and Impacts Management (ESRM)
Development of Environmental and Social Management Systems (ESMS)
Environmental and Social Assessments (ESIA, EIA)
Environmental and Social Management Plans (ESMPs)
Environmental and Social Audits (ESAs)
Environmental and Social Management Frameworks (ESMFs)
Occupational, Health and Safety (OHS)
Trainings and advice in environmental and social due diligence

ASSIGNMENTS EXECUTED

The ESSERM Consult team successfully carried out the following projects:

Reviewed the Enviromental and Social Management Systems (ESMSs) for Praise Export Company Limited, Ghana;
Reviewed the ESMSs for Gokay Mining Company Limited, Ghana;
Developed Environmental and Social Management Systems for ELMINA Microfinance Company Limited;
Environmental and Social Management Plans for Northern Rural Growth Programme (NRGP);
Environmental and Social Management Plans (ESMPs) for Bonwire Tourist Centre;
Environmental and Social Management Plans for Kwame Nkrumah’s Home and First Burial Site.
